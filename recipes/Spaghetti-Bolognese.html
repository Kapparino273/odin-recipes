Spaghetti Bolognese

  • 8 oz (225g) spaghetti
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or ground turkey
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 1 can (14 oz / 400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) beef or vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) red wine (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)
  • Fresh basil leaves (for garnish)

    1. Prepare the Ingredients: Chop the onion, carrot, celery, and garlic. If using ground beef, break it into small pieces.

    2. Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set aside.

    3. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large skillet or saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, carrot, celery, and garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are softened and the onion is translucent.

    4. Brown the Meat: If using ground beef or turkey, add it to the skillet with the sautéed vegetables. Break up the meat using a spatula and cook until it's no longer pink and has browned.

    5. Add Tomatoes and Seasoning: Stir in the crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper. If using red wine, add it at this point. Allow the mixture to simmer for a few minutes.

    6. Simmer the Sauce: Pour in the beef or vegetable broth and stir to combine. Reduce the heat to low, cover the skillet or saucepan, and let the sauce simmer for about 20-30 minutes. This allows the flavors to meld together and the sauce to thicken.

    7. Cook the Pasta: While the sauce is simmering, you can reheat the cooked pasta by briefly placing it in a pot of boiling water. Drain well.

    8. Serve: To serve, place a portion of cooked spaghetti on each plate. Spoon the Bolognese sauce over the pasta. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves.

    9. Enjoy: Serve the Spaghetti Bolognese hot and enjoy a hearty and flavorful dinner!
